<p style="color:red;">Welcome to you, and also welcome to me!</p>

I'm a first-year CS Ph.D. student at the University of British Columbia, advised by <a href="https://peterchencyc.com/">Prof. Peter Yichen Chen</a>. 
Rooted in computer graphics, scientific computing, and generative AI, my research develops **scalable models** for physics-based simulation, scientific discovery, and **physically grounded** generative models.

I received my B.S. in Computation Mathematics from <a href="http://gcl.ustc.edu.cn/">Graphics & Geometric Computing Lab</a> at <a href="http://gcl.ustc.edu.cn/">University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)</a>, where I was very fortunate to be advised by <a href="http://staff.ustc.edu.cn/~lgliu/">Prof. Ligang Liu</a>.
